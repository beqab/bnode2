CryptoIEX

CryptoIEX is the premier Estonia-based cryptocurrency exchange platform created by the Blocks Node's team. The platform's back-end is written on Java language and the front-end is on React. CryptoIEX's bitcoin node is secured by a firewall and has limited access to it. For security reasons, every component is updating rapidly

WAVE

WAVE is the first digital asset management platform based on blockchain technology. Our developers team created secured blockchain platform for exchange BTC to WAVE and vice versa with just few clicks. They also developed blockchain wallet, where is enabled to convert WAVE tokens into WAVE coins

Arena

Arena is a multiplayer mobile game for iOS and Android operation systems. Blocks Node created the game's backend based on the blockchain. Arena’s gameplay represents complex and innovative solutions for the back-end works
